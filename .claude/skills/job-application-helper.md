## Cover Letter Length & Tone

### Length
- **3-4 paragraphs** of body text
- Opening: 3-5 sentences (role + high-level fit)
- Body paragraphs: 4-7 sentences each (specific experience with metrics)
- Closing: 2-3 sentences (alignment + eagerness)

### Tone
- Professional but conversational
- Confident without arrogance
- Specific with metrics
- Avoid generic platitudes
- Match company's communication style
- Show you've researched them (mission, values, tech stack)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't** use non-breaking hyphens (‑) - use regular hyphens (-)
2. **Don't** inflate or change metrics - keep them consistent
3. **Don't** claim experience you don't have (e.g., NestJS, tRPC, Elixir)
4. **Don't** be vague - use specific technologies and numbers
5. **Don't** write generic cover letters - tailor to company mission
6. **Don't** over-emphasize one area - balance based on role requirements
7. **Don't** forget signature path: `../signature.png`

## Resume vs Cover Letter Customization

### Resume Tailoring
- Reorder bullet points to put most relevant first
- Adjust Professional Summary emphasis
- Add/remove specific technology mentions
- Highlight relevant projects more prominently
- Keep all bullet points factual and unchanged in content
- **See `.claude/prompts/resume-customization.md` for detailed guidelines**

### Cover Letter Generation
- Write fresh prose (don't copy resume bullets)
- Tell a narrative arc across experience
- Connect experience to company mission
- Use their exact language from job description
- Show understanding of their product/platform
- Express genuine interest in their specific work
- **See `.claude/prompts/cover-letter-generation.md` for detailed guidelines**
